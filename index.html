1960

The conflict intensified in the 1960's, when CIA-trained counter terror squads disappeared and killed thousands of people in the eastern part of the country.

In the seventies, repression accelerated, especially under the presidency of Carlos Arana Osorio, known as the Butcher of Zacapa for his role in the early counter-insurgency.

The peak of the conflict happened in the early 1980's, under the presidency of Rios Montt.
